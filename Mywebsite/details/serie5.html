ARROW

Release Date : 2012

Type : Action / Adventure / Crime

Seasons : 8

Synopsis : Spoiled billionaire playboy Oliver Queen is missing and presumed dead when his yacht is lost at sea. He returns five years later a changed man, determined to clean up the city as a hooded vigilante armed with a bow.

Stars :

  • Stephen Amell
  • Katie Cassidy
  • Katie Cassidy

Movie Reviews :

Reviewed by SoulR34p3r :

The first 2 seasons of the show was amazing however after that it got stale and boring. I decided to give up less than half way through season 5. The flashbacks become uninteresting. The villains after 2 seasons are boring. Love story is soap opera material. Season 4 made the show unrealistic with the sci-fi magic. Arrow was more interesting when he was working alone without any sidekicks.

Reviewed by AJin147 :

I had been a big fan when Arrow came out. The first two seasons were really powerful. However, what came after that was forgetful and just basic. I cannot even remember what the hell happened after season 2.
