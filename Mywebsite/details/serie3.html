Release Date : 2010

Type : Horror / Drama / Thriller

Seasons : 11

Synopsis : Sheriff Deputy Rick Grimes wakes up from a coma to learn the world is in ruins, and must lead a group of survivors to stay alive.

Stars :

  • Andrew Lincoln
  • Norman Reedus
  • Melissa McBride
